Who is Bassel?

  • Bassel was born in Damascus, Syria in 1981. His father is Palestinian.

    Bassel 1981年出生於敘利亞大馬士革。他的父親是巴勒斯坦人。

  • Bassel learned computers from his uncle when he was young.

    Bassel在很小年紀就開始跟叔叔學習電腦方面的知識。

Who is Bassel?

  • Bassel taught himself a lot about programming, and later studied computer science at university.

    Bassel 透過自學，學會了程式設計，並於大學時主修電腦科學。

  • Bassel worked as a computer professional in Syria. Using the Internet, he also volunteered for free software projects all over the world.

    Bassel在敘利亞是一名專業的電腦工程師。透過網路，他也以志願者身分加入世界各地的自由軟體運動。

Who is Bassel?

  • Bassel worked on an important, free culture site called Open Clip Art Library.

    Bassel 長期為一個重要的自由軟體文化網站 Open Clip Art Library 工作。

  • In 2009, Bassel helped launch Creative Commons Syria.

    2009年，Bassel 協助「敘利亞創用CC」組織的設立。

Who is Bassel?

  • Bassel was invited all over the world to give speeches and workshops.

    Bassel 受邀到世界各地演講、舉辦工作坊。

  • When the revolution in Syria began, Bassel chose to stay there to help his family and friends.

    在敘利亞展開革命行動之際，Bassel選擇留在家鄉，和家人、朋友一起奮戰。
